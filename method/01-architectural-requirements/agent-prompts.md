# Agent Prompts - Architectural Requirements

## Intake

```text
Read project-brief.md and 01-architectural-requirements/input.md. Extract business goal, scope, stakeholders, constraints, external systems, glossary, risks, and open questions. Do not propose architecture yet.
```

## ASR

```text
Generate the ASR section of architecture-drivers.md. Detect architecturally significant requirements from project-brief.md and input.md. Classify them as key functionality, quality attribute, constraint, or concern. Mark ambiguities and non-measurable requirements.
```

## QAW-lite

```text
Complete architecture-drivers.md: convert quality attributes into measurable scenarios, propose a utility tree and primary/supporting driver list by business importance, technical difficulty, and risk. Make explicit the metrics and tradeoffs the architect must approve.
```

## Generate Architecture Drivers

```text
Using project-brief.md and 01-architectural-requirements/input.md, generate a complete architecture-drivers.md. Include intake summary, ASR classification, quality attribute scenarios, utility tree, approved-driver proposal, and open questions. Mark the document as generated by agent and reviewed/approved by architect.
```
